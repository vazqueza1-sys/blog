Stop Giving Michelangelo a Q-Tip

You wouldn’t ask Michelangelo to paint the Sistine Chapel with a Q-tip. Or send your fire department into a burning building with squirt guns.

Absurd, right?

And yet—that’s what a lot of workers are dealing with every single day. Outdated systems. Broken tools. Long-winded processes designed to work around the limitations of in-house technology.

This isn’t about laziness or lack of talent. It’s about the reality on the ground—especially in local government.

Your people want to do good work. But they’re stuck trying to meet modern expectations with tools that weren’t built for the job.

Let’s fix that.
